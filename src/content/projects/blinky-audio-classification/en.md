## From sound to light

This research investigates acoustic event classification through **Blinky**, using a pre-trained autoencoder to translate audio representations into light-domain signals.

The work began with a sound-to-light conversion study presented at the 2024 IEICE General Conference and developed into a pre-training approach presented at APSIPA ASC 2025.

## Research arc

- Sound-to-light conversion using a pre-trained autoencoder
- Acoustic event classification through translated representations
- A reproducible implementation for continued experimentation

The linked repository contains the research source code. A future web demo can pair source audio, converted representations, and classifier outputs on one timeline.
