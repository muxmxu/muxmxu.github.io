## Research question

Voice conversion usually asks *who* a voice should sound like. This project also asks *where* that converted voice should be heard.

My doctoral research explores representations and learning methods for spatial voice conversion, connecting speech transformation with spatial audio understanding and rendering.

## Current direction

The work is currently in progress, with a focus on converted speech across varied spatial arrangements.
